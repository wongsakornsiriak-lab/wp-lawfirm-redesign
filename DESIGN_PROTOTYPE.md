## 1. Visual Identity

### Color Palette (Enhanced Green Theme - Modern & Premium)
- **Primary (Brand Anchor):** Deep Forest Green `#1B5E20` - Trust, stability, professional identity
- **Primary Medium:** Medium Green `#2E7D32` - Hover states, active elements
- **Primary Light:** Light Green `#4CAF50` - Accents, highlights, fresher feel
- **Secondary:** Gold `#C9A227` - Premium, luxury, CTA buttons
- **Light Green:** `#E8F5E9` - Soft backgrounds, cards, sections
- **Background:** Off-white `#FAFBFC` / Pure white `#FFFFFF`
- **Text:** Dark charcoal `#1F2937` / Gray `#6B7280`

### Gradient Options
- Primary gradient: `linear-gradient(135deg, #1B5E20 0%, #2E7D32 100%)`
- CTA gradient: `linear-gradient(135deg, #C9A227 0%, #D4AF37 100%)`
- Background gradient: `linear-gradient(180deg, #E8F5E9 0%, #FFFFFF 100%)`

### Typography
- **Headings:** "Playfair Display" - Elegant, authoritative
- **Body:** "Inter" - Clean, readable, modern
- **Accent:** "DM Sans" - Professional, versatile

## Design Inspiration: Fritz Law (fritzlawstl.com)

### Key Elements to Incorporate:

1.  **"Simple & Stress-Free" Positioning**
    *   Focus on making the process "easy" and "streamlined".
    *   Slogan: "A simple approach to [Practice Area]".

2.  **Clean, PaperStreet-style Design**
    *   Large hero image (landscape photo).
    *   Large, centered headline with elegant typography.
    *   Minimalist color palette (White background, Dark Navy/Charcoal text, Accent color).
    *   Ample whitespace.

3.  **Visual Hierarchy & Typography**
    *   Serif font for headings (Playfair Display, Lora, or Merriweather).
    *   Sans-serif for body (DM Sans, Inter, or Open Sans).
    *   High contrast text.

4.  **Stats Bar**
    *   Clean counters (e.g., "0+ 5-Star Reviews", "0M+ Assets Distributed").

5.  **Process Section**
    *   Numbered steps (Step 1, Step 2...) illustrating the workflow.
    *   "Faster. Simpler. Easier."

6.  **Team Section**
    *   Professional headshots.
    *   Name, Title, and a short bio snippet.
    *   "Meet the Team" header.

7.  **Testimonials**
    *   Google review style integration.
    *   User avatar, name, date, and quote.

8.  **Resources / Lead Magnet & Contact Section**
    *   **Dual Column Layout:**
        *   Left: "Free Legal Guide" download form (White background, elegant).
        *   Right: "Contact Us" form (Dark Green background, elegant).
    *   **Visuals:** Use contrasting backgrounds to make both forms stand out yet feel cohesive.

9.  **FAQ Section**
    *   Accordion style for common questions.

10. **Blog Section**
    *   Grid of recent articles with featured images.

11. **Location / Map**
    *   Embedded Google Maps showing office location.
    *   Contact details next to map.

12. **Footer**
    *   Detailed contact info, social links, and legal disclaimers.
