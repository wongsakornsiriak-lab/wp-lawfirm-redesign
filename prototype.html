Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to hire a lawyer?
We offer a free initial consultation to discuss your case. Many of our cases are handled on a contingency fee basis, meaning you don't pay unless we win.
How long will my case take?
The timeline varies depending on the complexity of the case and court schedules. We strive to resolve matters as efficiently as possible.
Do you handle cases outside of Bangkok?
Yes, we provide nationwide legal services throughout Thailand.
What should I bring to my first consultation?
Please bring any relevant documents, such as contracts, police reports, insurance papers, and identification.
